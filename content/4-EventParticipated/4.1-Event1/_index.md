# FCAJ Community Day 2026

### Event Objectives

- Create a collaborative space for networking, knowledge sharing, and inspiration among members of the tech community.
- Share practical knowledge, emerging trends, and real-world production experiences from experts in Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence (AI), DevOps, and Platform Engineering.
- Shape the mindset and essential skills required for IT professionals (especially students and interns) in response to rapid fluctuations in both local and global labor markets.

### Speakers

- **Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung** – Solution Architect at AWS Vietnam & Founder of FCAJ
- **Mr. Tinh Truong** – Platform Engineer at Gotamic (Gotam X)
- **Mr. Hai Anh** – Young Tech Speaker at Pacific Vietnam
- **Mr. Nguyen Han Thinh** – DevOps Engineer
- **Ms. Uyen & Ms. Thao** – Tech Engineers, Winners of Los H Hackathon (Project UTMMorpho)
- **Expert from VPBank** – Enterprise Specialist in Platform and AI deployment

### Key Highlights

#### The Market Landscape & AI Trends (Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung)

- AI significantly lowers software development costs, causing the demand for building software to skyrocket.  
- Emergence of new operational workflows, such as AI code fixing and Platform Engineering to manage massive systems.  

#### The Importance of Context in AI (Mr. Tinh Truong)

- Demystified “Context in AI” and addressed the "Internet Buller" phenomenon (over-reliance on randomly pulling internet resources).  
- To deliver accurate outputs, engineers must feed AI specific project-level context rather than generic knowledge or overly long prompts.  

#### Amazon Q Apps & Agent (Mr. Hai Anh)

- Demoed transforming raw data (e.g., Excel sheets) into automated BI analytics dashboards.  
- Built smart autonomous Agents capable of taking actions (e.g., summarizing meetings and automatically sending emails).  

#### AWS CloudFront Flat Rate Pricing (Mr. Nguyen Han Thinh)

- Architectural solutions protecting businesses from "Bill Spikes" caused by DDoS attacks or sudden traffic surges.  
- CloudFront compresses data up to 82%, reducing CPU load for backend EC2 instances while bolstering security through mTLS and VPC Origin.  

#### The 36-Hour Journey to a Hackathon Victory (Ms. Uyen & Ms. Thao)

- Shared the intense journey of building UTMMorpho (an AI app that generates responsive HTML/CSS layouts from images with drag-and-drop editing).  
- Navigated challenges under high fatigue and token exhaustion boundaries.  

#### Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent Systems (VPBank Expert)

- Dissected the business logic of credit scoring models for startups.  
- Orchestrated a cluster of specialized domain Agents (Financial, Market, Team, Risk) under a master Orchestrator to solve complex transactions.  

### Key Takeaways

#### Hard Skills

- **Optimizing the Context Window**: Condense input payload and decouple complex monolithic tasks into separate execution branches.  
- **Mastering LLM Execution Behaviors**: LLMs are probabilistic models; temperature = 0 does not guarantee 100% deterministic outputs. Downstream services must incorporate continuous verification.  
- **Cloud Cost Governance**: Predictable pricing (CloudFront Flat Rate) eliminates budgeting risks from traffic anomalies or external security threats.  
- **Production Multi-Agent Systems**: Structure specialized personas under a central Orchestrator node to automate comprehensive enterprise workflows.  

#### Mindset & Soft Skills

- **Shift from “Demo” to “Production-Ready”**: Build real production systems that solve industry-specific domain problems rather than basic academic demos.  
- **Supremacy of Security & Compliance**: AI in regulated enterprise environments requires strict privacy boundaries and Audit Trails.  
- **Focusing on the Core Pain Point**: Avoid over-engineering under tight deadlines; solve one single, practical pain point exceptionally well.
